About ML March

ML March is a comprehensive one-month sprint focusing on Machine Learning organized collaboratively by CodeIIEST - The Official Coding Club of IIEST Shibpur and Google Developer Students Club (GDSC) @IIEST.

The program is designed to take participants from the basics of Data Science to advanced topics in Machine Learning. Through hands-on workshops and expert-led sessions, participants will gain practical experience in Neural Networks, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Robotics, and MLOps.

Whether you're a beginner looking to start your ML journey or an experienced developer wanting to expand your knowledge, ML March offers valuable insights and practical skills to advance your career in the field of Artificial Intelligence.

Session Schedule

Week 1

Introduction to Data Science and Statistical Methods for ML

Instructors: Sarah Jane Topno & Suchana Hazra

A foundational overview of key statistical methods and data science principles essential for machine learning.

Week 1

Introduction to Machine Learning

Instructors: Harsh Raj Gupta, Sayan Roy, Arkapravo Das

Explore core ML concepts, algorithms, and implementation techniques.

Week 2

Implementation of Machine Learning Models and Applications

Instructors: Angshuman Roy, Drup Acharya, Subhrajit Deb

Hands-on session on implementing various ML models and exploring real-world applications.

Week 2

Introduction to Artificial Neural Networks

Instructors: Angshuman Roy, Drup Acharya, Subhrajit Deb, Sanju De

Deep dive into neural network architectures, training methodologies, and applications.

Week 3

Introduction to Natural Language Processing

Instructors: Soham Haldar, Sarah Jane Topno

Learn how machines understand, interpret, and generate human language.

Week 3

Computer Vision

Instructors: Drup Acharya, Sanju De, Angshuman Roy, Soham Haldar, Subhranil Nandy, Subhrajit Deb

Discover techniques for image analysis, object detection, and visual recognition systems.

Week 4

Robotics

Instructor: Sanju De

Explore the integration of ML techniques in robotics and autonomous systems.

Week 4

MLOps

Instructor: Swastika Kar

Learn best practices for deploying, managing, and scaling machine learning models in production.
